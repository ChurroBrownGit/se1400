ABOUT

What are we about? What can I do?

 

This website was created in 2025 as part of a Web Development project for my 11th grade class. While it started as a school assignment, it quickly turned into something more fun and personal. I wanted to build a site that not only helped me learn how to code, but also gave people something to smile about. And what better way to do that than with cats?

Cats are basically the internet’s favorite animal. They’re funny, dramatic, lazy, chaotic, and completely adorable. Whether it’s a cat sleeping in a shoe, chasing invisible objects, or sitting in a perfectly-sized box, they have a way of making people laugh and feel better. That’s what this site is all about—celebrating those weird and wonderful cat moments.

You’ll find a few different pages around the site:

  • Contact Page – If you have questions, suggestions, or just want to say hi, you can find my email here. I’ll try to respond as soon as possible.
  • Upload Page – Want to share your own cat photo? You can upload it here! All images are moderated before being added to the site to keep everything safe and positive.
  • Gallery Page – This is the main hub for cat photos. Browse through images submitted by others, or check back to see if your own photo made it in!
  • Cat of the Week (COTW) – Each week, one featured cat is chosen and users can vote. It’s just for fun, but we treat it like serious business.
  • Home Page – Get a quick overview of the site and see a few highlighted images with captions to help you get started.

This website is a mix of code, creativity, and community. I built it to learn something new and to create something that makes people happy, even for just a moment. Whether you're just here to browse or want to take part, I hope you enjoy it.

Thanks for visiting, and don’t forget to give your cat a high-five from me.

You're awesome!

- Joel Hadley
